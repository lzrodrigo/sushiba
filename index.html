What is the Internet of Assets (IoA)?

The Internet of Assets is the next evolution of how we perceive the internet in our daily lives. It is the logical next step from the previous implementation of The Internet of Things (IoT). The Internet of Assets refers to the direct and provable ownership of internet assets

The Internet of Assets can therefore be considered as an umbrella term that encompasses the modern advancements of blockchain integrated digitalization.
